Books Clubs

A book club is a reading group where people agree to read the same book and meet regularly to discuss it.

All books

Book clubs are actively reading and discussing a mix of fresh releases and modern classics. Some of the most widely discussed titles include:Intermezzo by Sally RooneyThe Boyfriend by Freida McFaddenBeautiful Ugly by Alice FeeneyOne Golden Summer by Carley Fortune

Fiction

Fiction books are works of literature that feature imaginary characters, settings, and events. Rather than reporting real-world facts or true stories, they are born from the author's creativity to entertain, tell a story, or explore deeper themes

Science

A science book is a publication that explores scientific topics, theories, experiments, or discoveries. Grounded in empirical evidence, these works aim to inform, educate, or entertain readers about scientific phenomena
