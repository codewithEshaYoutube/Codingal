    • Kids Health

    Child health is the overall physical, mental, and social well-being of children from birth through adolescence. It encompasses not just the absence of disease, but the ability to develop physical, cognitive, and emotional potential, satisfy basic needs, and successfully interact with their environments.

    Tips

    1. Balanced NutritionFocus on Whole Foods: Incorporate plenty of colorful vegetables, fresh or dried fruits, whole grains, and lean proteins (like poultry, fish, eggs, beans, and nuts).Choose Water: Swap out sugary juices and sodas for water to keep them hydrated.Make it Fun: Let your kids pick out new produce at the grocery store or involve them in preparing meals to encourage healthy eating.

    2. Active MovementAim for an Hour: Children should get at least 60 minutes of moderate to vigorous physical activity every day.Make it a Family Affair: Go for evening walks, ride bikes, or visit local parks and playgrounds.Enroll in Activities: Sign them up for organized sports or dance classes.
